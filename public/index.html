About Sunil

I want to Move Troy Forward Together. The City is getting split up right now with politics being played out in the open. I strongly believe we can all work together in a collaborative fashion. I am not here to make a name for myself, but am here to be the voice of the Citizens of Troy. I have been involved with our community in various capabilities like

  • City of Troy Traffic Committee Member
  • City of Troy Finance and Incentive Team Member
  • Troy Youth Assistance Board Member
  • Troy Historic Village Board Member (Trustee)
  • Troy School District Facilities Board member 2015-2016
